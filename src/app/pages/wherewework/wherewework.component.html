Around the world

Since PGC of Hope was established, help and hope have been delivered to hurting people in more than 127 countries. PGC currently serves communities by empowering women, feeding kids, and helping farmers yield larger crops. A global network — which includes two regional offices in Europe and Australia — strengthens PGC’s ability to respond to natural disasters, extreme poverty, and hunger.
