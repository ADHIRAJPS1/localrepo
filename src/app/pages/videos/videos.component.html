2Gen Parent Testimonials from Horizons Unlimited Christian Academy

The Community Foundation engaged in a three year, collaborative pilot with Horizons Unlimited Christian Academy that included support for teachers, students, and their parents. Upon the conclusion of the pilot, parents shared how they felt they had a greater capacity to create a better future for their families.

(May 2021, 8 Minutes)
