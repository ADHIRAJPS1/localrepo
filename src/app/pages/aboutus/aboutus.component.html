Transforming Healthcare In Every Possible Way.

{{ config.appDisplayName }} is a Humanity Welfare Council initiative that aims to transform the health care sector in India by providing necessary funds to people in urgent need of medical assistance and advancing the medical system with increased accessibility. Our motto is to transform our health system and make medical care available for the general public without any burden of extra cost. Access to healthcare is a basic human right that every citizen should be able to afford.

Our team comprises highly driven and committed individuals dedicated to creating a developed and healthy world. And in our race towards a developed India, the health sector is our priority. Our initiative symbolizes our philanthropist nature, and we have been making a profound difference in the lives of many.

The healthcare system in our country is fighting various problems, including the insufficient number of institutions and short-staffed human resources. A few people get expensive, world-class care, while a large part of the population doesn't even get the primary quality care. Over 60% of our population pays out of pocket for healthcare, driving millions into poverty.

A healthy human is the best resource for any nation. They are the basis of the development of a country. Only if a person is physically and mentally fit, he thinks beyond his zone and works for the upliftment of the nation. As the saying goes, a healthy body is a temple where a healthy mind resides.

We focus on creating a strong, robust and healthy environment as a progressive approach towards the developing world. We strive to provide medical access for every individual and build a disease-free human race. Therefore, we are creating a broader platform where everyone can easily reach in case of a medical emergency without any unnecessary formalities and delayed time in attending to their medical concerns.

On this platform, we also aim to unite the medical researchers, medical institutions, government and non-government bodies under one roof to attain solutions to globally arising medical concerns. We are also working on creating advanced and high medical solutions at affordable costs.

We maintain transparency regarding our daily fund operations and assistance services. However, we hold ourselves liable to our trusted donors and people who approach us for help.

Your time and skills could help us in saving a precious life!

Our Mission & Vision

Making healthcare accessible to all and transforming health sector to make India healthy by 2030

Mission

We aim to create innovative technology and finance solutions to empower Indians concerning healthcare. We create a system to raise funds for medical emergencies, critical illnesses, and a range of social and personal causes. Lakhs have used our platform to change lives through timely contributions, fundraisers and auctions.

Vision

We envision a country where medical care is available and accessible for every citizen without any burden of extra costs. We aim to reconstruct the healthcare system in India by providing necessary funds to people in desperate need of medical assistance and bringing advancement in the medical system.

Meet Our Team
Surya Pratap Singh (Administrative Director)

Mr. Surya Pratap Singh is the founder of Humanity Welfare Council and the Director of Project Global Cure, which aims at transforming the health care sector of India. He is a visionary who builds bold start-ups and happy families. He is an idea aficionado and makes ideas turn into reality. He loves working with people and is a man of hope, dedicated to bringing about a change for a better tomorrow.

Surya Pratap Singh has set up many business verticals aimed at helping people. He dreams of a healthy, happy and disease-free India. With a vision to build a platform that would cater to all the medical requirements of the people of India, Project Global Cure was launched on 13th November 2020.

Ms. Udita Saikia (Co-Founder)

Udita is a young and enthusiastic social worker who genuinely cares for society and wants to make it better in every possible way. She holds a Master's degree in Social Work from Amity University and has gained valuable work experience working for many major welfare organizations in different parts of the country. She takes a positive approach to every situation and connects well with people from diverse backgrounds and age groups. She builds a bond of trust and wins hearts with her dedication to work. She says, "Since I was a child, helping others has given me immense satisfaction. The caring attitude and my compassionate nature certainly inspired me to choose this career as a social worker." She quotes - "A change starts from you so, be the change you wish to see in the world."
