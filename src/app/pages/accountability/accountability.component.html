How Can You Help?

Our Monthly Donation:

You can contribute a tiny share of your income every month to our monthly fund collection. At the time of a medical emergency, this fund can be used to support families. By donating a small amount, you can create a big difference in the lives of many people.

Our contingency fund for medical emergencies provides financial support to those going through medical emergencies whose financial condition is not good enough to support them sustain such emergencies.

We understand that by providing financial support, we can't resolve all the issues. So we also take care of pre-medical and post-medical care, especially for the families if the sole earning member is going through a medical condition or death. We monitor the patient for a year before we close the case.
